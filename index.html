The gender pay gap in sports

How bad is the gender pay gap in professional sport? In which sports do women and men have a similar remuneration? And which ones still have an enormous difference? For this visualization, we took a look at the earnings of the top 20 male and female professional players for 2019 in three sports: Basketball, Golf and Tennis. Only the money earned on tour (for golf and tennis) or as a salary (for basketball) has been taken into account, leaving aside endorsement deals.
